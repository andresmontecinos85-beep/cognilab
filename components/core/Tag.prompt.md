Small pill label for categories, modalities of attention, or specialty areas.

```jsx
<Tag tone="primary">Fonoaudiología</Tag>
<Tag tone="accent" dot>A domicilio</Tag>
<Tag tone="outline">Jardines y colegios</Tag>
```

Tones: `primary` · `accent` · `amber` · `neutral` · `outline`. Pass `dot` for a leading status dot.
